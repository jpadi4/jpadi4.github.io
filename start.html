UIUC | Summer 2024 | Li Padilla

Expected vs. Excess Deaths During the COVID-19 Pandemic

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic drastically halted all "regular" life. This narrative visualization aims to emphasize how COVID altered aspects of life, more specifically, yearly average mortality. The data is not exhaustive past 2020, but this narrative aims to illustrate not only the severity of the early stages of the pandemic but also shows how the abrupt rise of COVID makes the predictive models diverge from reality.

The excess deaths dataset featured is part of the New York times COVID-19 data.
